AI Agent

Tirza — My AI Job Search Agent

Named after my cat 🐈, Tirza is an autonomous multi-agent pipeline that scans LinkedIn, AllJobs, Comeet, and Drushim three times a day. She scores job listings, deduplicates them, and delivers a ranked HTML report — all while I'm doing other things. Built with OpenClaw and Claude API. Scans 4 job boards · 3× daily · zero doomscrolling.
